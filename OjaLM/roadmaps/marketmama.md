# MarketMama AI Roadmap
**Commerce Operating System**

MarketMama AI is the operating system that delivers the entire Oja AI Research stack to everyday users. It is the flagship product powered by MamaPrice, expanding far beyond a simple chat interface to include voice, maps, payments, delivery integrations, and B2B dashboards.

## Current Focus: v1.0 Platform Beta
- [ ] Build the core User Interface (Web/Mobile).
- [ ] Implement the "Ask MamaPrice" chat interface.
- [ ] Integrate user accounts, saved lists, and price alerts.

## Future Milestones
### v2.0: Vendor Discovery
- Allowing users to discover and verify physical vendors in local markets (e.g., Balogun, Bodija) directly through the platform.

### v3.0: B2B Integration
- Expanding MarketMama to serve wholesale buyers, restaurants, and logistics providers.
