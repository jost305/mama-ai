# MamaPrice Scout System

Scouts are trusted data collectors. They provide the ground-truth intelligence that separates Oja AI Research from standard language models trained on the open internet.

## The Moat
Data collected by Scouts is proprietary. It is the primary engine behind the **Trust Tiers**. A Price Observation collected by a Tier 1 Elite Scout carries mathematically higher weight during OjaData generation than a price scraped from an unverified e-commerce site.

## Verification
Every scout must be algorithmically vetted to prevent fraud. The system tracks:
- `GPS Variance`: Are they physically at the market they claim to be?
- `Dispute Rate`: How often is their data corrected by other scouts?
- `Device Info`: Is the data coming from a known, non-emulated device?

## The Scout Lifecycle
- **Applicant:** Low trust (Tier 5).
- **Verified:** Identity confirmed (Tier 4).
- **Active:** 100+ accurate submissions (Tier 2).
- **Elite:** Top 1% performance (Tier 1).
