# AfriqueQwen Candidate Profile

## Technical Specifications
- **Architecture:** Qwen-based
- **Parameters:** ~1.8B / 7B variants
- **Context Window:** 32k
- **License:** Open (Research/Commercial depending on base)
- **Primary Languages:** English, French, Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo

## Lab C Evaluation Matrix
- **Hardware Requirements:** Low (Runnable on consumer GPUs)
- **Quantization Support:** GGUF, AWQ compatible
- **Tool-Calling Support:** Moderate

## Hypothesis
**Strengths:** Expected to dominate the language analysis (C4) on Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo due to its dedicated African pre-training corpus.
**Weaknesses:** Might struggle with complex Commerce Math (C3) or deep multi-step Shopping Reasoning compared to larger foundation models.
