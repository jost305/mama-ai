# Trust Scoring Architecture

Every Knowledge Unit (KU) and Price Observation ingested into Oja AI Research must be scored. OjaLM does not treat all data equally. 

## The 5 Trust Tiers
- **Tier 1 (Highest Trust):** Official government statistics (e.g., NBS), manufacturer catalogs, Elite Scouts.
- **Tier 2 (High Trust):** Verified formal retailers, POS system APIs, Active Scouts.
- **Tier 3 (Medium Trust):** Verified market surveys, Applicant Scouts.
- **Tier 4 (Medium/Low Trust):** Community reports, unverified user-submitted receipts.
- **Tier 5 (Lowest Trust):** Social media, rumors, unverified WhatsApp forwards.

## Propagation
Trust scores propagate through the graph. A `Price Observation` generated by a Tier 1 Scout assigns a temporary Tier 1 trust bump to the associated `Vendor` and `Market` nodes. If a Tier 5 source disputes a Tier 1 source, the engine automatically ignores the dispute unless supported by 10+ independent Tier 3+ sources.
